Achievements

Best Emerging University in South India

Joy University has been honored with the prestigious "Best Emerging University in South India" award at the Pride of Nation Awards 2023. The award ceremony, organized by Asia Today Research & Media, took place on 30th September 2023 at Hotel ITC Kakatiya, Hyderabad.

The accolade was presented by Dr. Tamilisai Soundararajan, Governor of Telangana & Puducherry, to Dr. S.A. Joy Raja, Chancellor of Joy University and Chairman of the Rajas Group of Institutions. This recognition highlights the university's commitment to excellence in education and its remarkable contributions to shaping the future of higher learning in South India.

Titans of Tamil Nadu Award for Excellence

Joy University has been conferred with the prestigious Galata Media Awards – "Titans of Tamil Nadu" for Excellence in Promoting Higher Education – Science & Technology under the category of Quality Education, Best Infrastructure & Student Amenities. The award ceremony was held at Kalaivanar Arangam, Chennai, and was graced by the Honourable Minister for Dairy Development, Mr. Mano Thangaraj on June 7, 2025. The event saw the participation of around 1000 higher secondary students, parents, and educators from Government and Government-aided institutions.
