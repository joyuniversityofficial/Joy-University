Prof. Dr. A. John De Britto obtained his Degree of Doctor of Philosophy from Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai. He has completed 34 years of teaching including 28 years of research in St.Xavierâ€™s College (Autonomous), Palayamkottai. He has completed 14 Major Projects and 7 Minor projects funded by TNSCST, UGC, DST, CSIR, ICMR, MOEF, PWD, and ICSSR which include both scientific and socially relevant projects.
