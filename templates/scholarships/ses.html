JU Talent Quest Scholarship

An Exclusive Opportunity to Earn Merit-Based Scholarships!

Joy University proudly announces the Scholastic Excellence Scholarship Exam (JU-SES) 2025, an exclusive opportunity for aspiring students to secure up to 50% tuition fee waiver for their entire course duration of UG Courses at JU!

JU-SES is one-of-a-kind merit-based scholarship that recognizes and rewards academic brilliance. This scholarship is separate from the existing scholarships listed on the university website, offering students a chance to secure up to 50% tuition fee waiver for their entire UG course duration!

Talent Exam Registration

Exam Dates:

  • 1st Test: 30th April 2025
  • 2nd Test: 20th May 2025

Why is JU-SES Special?

  • Exclusive Merit-Based Scholarship: This is an extraordinary opportunity to secure financial support based purely on academic excellence.
  • Independent of Other Scholarships: Students can still apply for other existing scholarships at Joy University as per eligibility.
  • Multiple Chances to Win: Appear in either of both exam dates for a better percentile ranking.

Eligibility & Benefits:

  • Students ranked as per percentile-based merit order will be awarded a 50% tuition fee waiver for the entire course duration (4/3 years as per program)
  • Students meeting the minimum percentile but not securing a scholarship will be placed on a waiting list, making them eligible for other university scholarships.
  • Special consideration for a one-time fixed fee waiver may be granted to waiting list candidates who do not qualify for other scholarship categories.
